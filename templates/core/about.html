About

Craft & Intelligence

My studio represents the fusion of traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge artificial intelligence. For over 12 years, I've been creating detailed 3D models of medals, coins, and bas-reliefs, while simultaneously exploring the frontiers of AI-generated imagery and video.

My work bridges the gap between classical artistry and modern technology. Each 3D model begins with careful research into historical context, followed by meticulous digital sculpting. Meanwhile, AI tools allow me to visualize concepts and explore creative directions that would be impossible through traditional methods alone.

Whether you need a custom commemorative medal, want to explore AI-generated concept art, or are looking for a collaborator on a creative project, I'm here to help bring your vision to life.
